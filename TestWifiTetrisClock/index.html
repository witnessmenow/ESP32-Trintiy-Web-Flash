WiFi Tetris Clock

A clock with falling tetris blocks for your ESP32 Trinity.

Once installed it, will create a WiFi access point:
SSID: WifiTetris
pass: clock123

From here you can configure the clock.
NOTE: To enter config mode again, press the reset button, and while there is text on the screen, press it a second time


NOTE: Make sure to close anything using your devices com port (e.g. Serial monitor)
Your browser does not support the Web Serial API.
